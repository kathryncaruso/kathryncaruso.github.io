Professional Summary

I study applications of cold-adapted bacterial isolates for biocementation in permafrost environments at Montana State University’s Center for Biofilm Engineering. My research spans microbiology, bioengineering, ecology, and remote sensing, with a foundation in open science and reproducible methods.
