26 g powder · 500 mL water

Part A: Prepare BHI Agar Base

~2 hours
  • 1
    Suspend powder
    ▶
    Suspend 26 g of Difco™ BHI Agar powder in 500 mL of purified water.
    Fill bottle to no more than 50% capacity to allow room for boiling.
  • 2
    Mix thoroughly
    ▶
    Mix powder and water thoroughly to ensure even suspension before heating.
  • 3
    Heat & boil
    1 min
    ▶
    Heat with frequent agitation and boil for 1 minute to completely dissolve the powder.
  • 4
    Autoclave
    15 min
    ▶
    Autoclave at 121°C for 15 minutes.
  • 5
    Transfer to water bath
    ▶
    Immediately transfer to a 60°C water bath after autoclaving.
  • 6
    Equilibrate
    ≥ 15 min
    ▶
    Equilibrate for at least 15 minutes, or until the bottle is just cool enough to handle.
    Caution: Do not allow agar to cool below ~50°C or it will begin to solidify.

Part B: Pour Plates

~30 min
  • 7
    Agitate gently
    ▶
    Gently agitate the molten agar to distribute any precipitate uniformly.
  • 8
    Pour into plates
    ▶
    Pour into sterile petri dishes: fill each plate so the agar level sits just below the bottom of the lid when closed (~20–25 mL per standard 100 mm plate).
    Selected batch yields approximately ~20 plates.
  • 9
    Evaporate condensation
    ▶
    Leave lids slightly ajar to allow condensation to evaporate.
  • 10
    Solidify & sleeve
    ▶
    Once moisture has evaporated and plates have solidified, return them to the sleeve.
